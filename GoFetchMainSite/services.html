Why Your Business Needs a Professional Website

SAVES MONEY

Many people think that websites are too costly and you can't afford them. The truth is you can't afford NOT to have a website. Compared to costs associated with other avenues of marketing such as magazine or newspaper ads, and the near limitless market you can reach, it can have a hugely beneficial impact on your return on investment.

SAVES TIME

A website can cut out a tremendous amount of the time it takes to inform people in your targeted audience about a new product, a new promotion, or if you're just getting started and are ready to find customers. Once your custom built website is up and running you can get everything you need to your customers in one simple place accessible on just about any device.

ALWAYS ACCESSIBLE

Unlike you, your website can be visited 24/7/365. It gives your current and potential customers access to any and all information you have listed about your products, services, or promotions.

SHOWCASE YOUR WORK

By listing pictures and information about yourself and/or your business as well as the products and services you are selling, you can really show off your work and how you differ from other similar businesses out there. Using things like customer testimonials, a FAQ page, or an image or video gallery, you can really start to separate yourself from the rest and show off how you are different and unique.

GAIN CREDIBILITY

More and more people use the internet every single day to find the products and services they need. Whether it is finding a babysitter, car mechanic, or a personal trainer, the internet is becoming many people's method of choice to search and compare the different choices that are out there.

Our Process

In order to build a product that is exactly what you're looking for, we keep you involved throughout the entire process. This avoids common issues such as delivering a first proof that is nothing like what you want, or delivering a final product that doesn't match your vision.

Introduction

We get get to know your business and what you hope to achieve with your website.
* What are your needs and wants and what are your goals for building an online presence?

Brainstorm

This is when we start sharing ideas.
* What are other websites that catch your attention?
* What is your target audience?
* What are key search words that will generate the most traffic?

Plan

This is when we start putting pen to paper. Based on your wants and needs based on the brainstorming phase, we begin to jot down the things that are most necessary first and work our way down to things that would be nice to have and so forth.

Design & Development

We start building up possible designs we think might appeal to you and your needs. We'll turn the designs you like into code.

Test and Preview

This is where we put our design and code to the test. We will test every part of your site across several different devices to ensure smooth responsiveness.

Launch

After your site has been thoroughly tested and has passed our rigorous tests, we will push your site up making it live so it can start generating traffic.

* Next 30 Days *

Over the next 30 days we offer free updates to your site. Updates might include adding similar pages or landing pages to help generate more traffic. Updates do not include a major redesign of the structure or layout. We will however offer a major redesign at a 50% reduced rate.

* Next 90 Days *

Over the next 90 days, we will check Google Analytics every couple of days to get a better idea of how your site is doing and what is generating the most traffic or causing a lack thereof. We offer free minor updates such as adding or changing any keywords or changing text on the site.

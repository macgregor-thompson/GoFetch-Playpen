Why Responsive Websites Are So Important

  • 25% of overall search queries are now on mobile devices
  • 80% of local searches on mobile devices turn into purchases
  • 42% of calls to businesses are driven by mobile search
  • 60% of smartphone users and 75% of tablet users search from their devices

Having a site designed so that your customers can easily access and utilize your site's features will greatly affect how your potential customers will react. Over 90% of a customer's first impression is design related. If your website is not easy to use, then you could be losing a great deal of potential customers for this reason alone.

At Go Fetch Web, we utilize the power of Bootstrap to make sure your site is ready to be seen on any device, large or small.
